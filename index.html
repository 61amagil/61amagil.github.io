Music Is Important

December 25, 2015

On this exciting day in December, I got an iPad Mini and it changed my life. I had heard music on the radio, but it never really stuck with me or interested me, until I got the iPad and found YouTube. Using YouTube, I discovered many artists that I took a liking for and realized that music is the thing that I needed most in this world. Music is what keeps me going, along with many others. If music didn't exist, many artists would not have been made seen. Never take music for granted! Thank you for your time.

One Of The Best Bands

Second Week Of September

Hurricane Dorian was aiming towards Savannah and I had just been put into a group chat named, "Weirdos." In this group chat there were a couple people who we'll call E and K, and E and K both shared a passion for the band called, "Twenty One Pilots." They talked about them often, especially E, and I decided to start listening to them. Best. Decision. Of. My. 13-year life. They're amazing and they are now my beans. Don't hurt them and keep your opinions to yourself! Thank you for your time.
